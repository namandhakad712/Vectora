About Vectora

Vectora is a lightweight fact-checking platform designed for accessible, offline-style verification support. We combine AI models, source-checking, and lineage analysis to provide users with a quick authenticity score and the reasoning behind it.

Our goal is to help people verify forwarded messages, social media claims, and suspicious images with minimal friction.
